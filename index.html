About

The person
behind the
credit models


Nick Halpern is the Chief Credit Officer of Square Financial Services, Block Inc.'s FDIC-insured industrial bank. Since joining Square in 2017, he has worked his way up from Data Scientist to Data Science Lead to the top credit chair—proof that if you crunch enough numbers, eventually you get to be the one who decides which numbers matter.

With a background spanning Bank of America, the Abu Dhabi Investment Authority, and Diversified Credit Investments, Nick brings a distinctly global perspective to Square's lending business. He has seen capital markets from Boston to Abu Dhabi, and somehow still chose San Francisco.

Armed with degrees in Mathematics, Economics, and Applied Statistics from Northwestern and Penn State, Nick is the rare finance executive who can explain a credit model in plain English—and then write the Python code for it himself.

When he's not stress-testing loan portfolios, you'll find him at the Berkeley Farmers Market (Saturday, 9am, do not be late), on a yoga mat, halfway through a 10-mile run that is "just a warmup," or tending to his backyard ducks with a mindfulness that is somewhat at odds with his other plans for them. He is a committed vegan. The ducks are a separate philosophical framework. He has paperwork.
